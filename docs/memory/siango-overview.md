**Siango (סיאנגו)** is a SaaS website/online-store builder for the Israeli market. Hebrew, RTL, Tailwind. Core promise: "build a live store/site in ~5 minutes" with AI assistance.

**People:**
- Owner: Moti - moti4384@gmail.com. Non-technical business owner. Self-describes as a "data analytics freak" who wants every possible metric.
- Partner: Daniel - furmand713@gmail.com. Gives design/UX feedback.
- Former developer: Eli - no longer involved ("פיתרתי אותו"). Claude now performs all the dev/ops tasks Eli used to do; do not wait on Eli for anything.

**Product flow:** merchant signs up (Google or email) -> AI-assisted onboarding (~8 steps: brand language source, category, hours, products, brand style, payments, publish) -> gets a storefront (products, cart, checkout, orders, payments) + a dashboard with analytics. 

**Business model:** Siango earns from a **monthly subscription, ₪69/mo**, billed via **iCount**. Merchant stores collect their own customer payments via **PayPlus**. As of 2026-06, there are **no paying customers yet** (pre-launch / about to market).

**Domains:** siango.app (marketing + app), send.siango.app (transactional email). Obfuscated admin route: `/manage-x7k9`.
